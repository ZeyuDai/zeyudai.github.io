I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Information Security Group at University College London (Computer Science Department). My area of research is cryptography and my supervisors are Professor Jens Groth and Dr. Emiliano De Cristofaro.

Before joining UCL, I received an MSc in Mathematics for Cryptography and Communication from Royal Holloway, University of London in 2013. In 2012, I received a BSc in Mathematics from the University of Turin.


[18.08.2018] I have recently been informed that a company called NIX added me to their list of collaborators. Please note that I was never involved in any of their projects.

Research Interests

I am interested in the development and deployment of cryptographic protocols. I am currently working on building efficient zero-knowledge proofs and arguments. My research interests includes lattice-based cryptography and multi-party computation protocols.
