I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University, under the supervision of Prof. Ruihong Huang. Before my Ph.D., I received my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Southern California. I am doing research in Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning.

My research interests lie specifically in the areas of information extraction, discourse parsing and machine learning. I am working on applying neural network models (including CNN, RNN, Transformer and Graph-based neural networks) to practical NLP problems and applications.
