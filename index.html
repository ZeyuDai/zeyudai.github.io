I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University, under the supervision of Prof. Ruihong Huang. I am working on Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. Before my Ph.D., I received my Bachelor's Degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and obtained my Master's Degree from University of Southern California respectively.

My research interests lie specifically in the areas of information extraction, discourse parsing and machine learning. I am interested in applying neural network models (including CNN, RNN, Transformer and Graph-based neural networks) to practical NLP problems and applications.
